# TerminalBench

A benchmark for evaluating AI coding agents on terminal-based tasks (version 2.0 referenced in sources).

## Notable Results

- [[LangChain]] moved from outside the top 30 to **rank 5** by changing only the [[Agent Harness]] infrastructure around the same model weights. A 20+ position swing purely from harness engineering.

This result is the primary empirical evidence that harness quality is a first-class performance lever independent of model capability.
